Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our clinic and services.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our billing department to verify if we accept your specific plan.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our main office at (123) 456-7890, using our online booking system, or by visiting our clinic in person. New patients are welcome.

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, list of current medications, medical records if available, and any relevant test results or imaging studies. Arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork.

Yes, we offer telemedicine consultations for many non-emergency medical issues. These virtual visits are conducted through our secure patient portal and are available for established patients.

Our regular clinic hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Emergency services are available 24/7.

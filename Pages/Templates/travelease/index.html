Our Story

Founded in 2012 by a group of passionate travelers, Wanderlust began as a small boutique agency specializing in customized European tours. What started as a shared love for authentic travel experiences has grown into a globally recognized travel company with operations in over 50 countries.

Today, we partner with the finest local guides, boutique hotels, and unique experiences to craft journeys that go beyond typical tourism. Our team of travel designers combines local expertise with a deep understanding of what makes travel truly transformative.
